# Lead Intake SOP - Local Liquidators

## Purpose
Standardize the process for receiving, processing, and routing all inbound leads to ensure consistent follow-up and maximum conversion.

## Process Flow

### Step 1: Lead Arrival (0-5 minutes)

**Automatic Actions:**
1. Intake server captures submission
2. DPS normalizes data
3. Contact record created
4. Auto-responder sent
5. Hub notification triggered

**Manual Verification:**
- Check for duplicate records
- Verify contact info completeness
- Flag any obvious spam/fake submissions

### Step 2: Lead Scoring (5-15 minutes)

**Score Based On:**
| Factor | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Timeline = ASAP | +30 |
| Timeline = 1-3 months | +20 |
| Property value $400K+ | +20 |
| Distress situation | +15 |
| Previous investor experience | +10 |
| Direct referral | +15 |
| Complete contact info | +10 |

**Priority Assignment:**
- 80-100 points: HOT → Immediate contact
- 50-79 points: WARM → Contact within 4 hours
- 20-49 points: STANDARD → Contact within 24 hours
- <20 points: NURTURE → Add to long-term sequence

### Step 3: Lead Routing (15-30 minutes)

**Assignment Rules:**
- Hot leads → Senior agent
- Distress situations → Specialist
- Buyer leads → Buyer agent
- Geographic zones → Area specialist
- Referrals → Referring agent's partner

**Notification:**
- SMS to assigned agent
- Email with lead summary
- Hub dashboard update

### Step 4: Initial Contact (Per priority timeline)

**Hot Leads (Within 30 min):**
1. Personal phone call
2. If no answer: SMS + voicemail
3. If still no answer: Escalate to manager

**Warm Leads (Within 4 hours):**
1. Phone call
2. SMS if no answer
3. Email with calendar link

**Standard Leads (Within 24 hours):**
1. Phone call
2. Follow-up SMS/email

### Step 5: Documentation (Immediately after contact)

**Update Contact Record:**
- Contact date/time
- Method used
- Outcome (connected, voicemail, no answer)
- Conversation notes
- Next action and date
- Status update (Hot/Warm/Cold)

## Quality Standards

- 100% of leads entered in CRM within 15 minutes
- 90% of hot leads contacted within 30 minutes
- 100% of leads have next action scheduled
- Zero leads without assigned owner after 1 hour
