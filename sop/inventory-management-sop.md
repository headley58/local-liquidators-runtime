# Inventory Management SOP - Local Liquidators

## Purpose
Track, categorize, and optimize inventory movement from acquisition through sale.

## Inventory Categories

| Category | Definition | Action Required |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| New | Acquired within 7 days | List within 48 hours |
| Featured | High-margin or desirable | Promote across channels |
| Fast Moving | Sells within 30 days | Replicate acquisition strategy |
| Standard | Normal velocity | Monitor weekly |
| Aging | 60+ days since listing | Price review + promotion |
| Clearance | 90+ days or distressed | Aggressive pricing/marketing |

## Process

### Acquisition
1. Property details entered within 24 hours
2. Photos uploaded (minimum 10)
3. Initial pricing analysis
4. Category assignment
5. Marketing plan created

### Weekly Review
1. Aging report generated
2. Price adjustments considered
3. Marketing intensity adjusted
4. Clearance candidates identified

### Monthly Analysis
1. Velocity by category
2. Margin analysis
3. Acquisition strategy refinement

## Inventory Tracking Fields

- Property ID
- Address
- Acquisition date
- Acquisition cost
- List price
- Current status (New/Featured/Aging/etc.)
- Days on market
- Marketing channels active
- Last price change
- Showing activity
- Offer history
